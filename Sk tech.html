ABOUT THE CO.

SK Tech Limited is an Indian multinational information technology company that
provides business consulting, informaion technology and outsourcing services. The
company is headquartered in Bangalore. SK Tech is the second-largest Indian IT
company after Tata Consultancy Services by 2020 revenue figures and the 62nd
largest public company in the world according to Forbes Global 2000 ranking. On 31
December 2020, its market capitalisation was $71.92 billion.
